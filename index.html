About Me

I earned my Ph.D. in Chemistry from Tsinghua University. From 2015 to 2021, I was a pivotal member of Professor Ruimao Hua’s laboratory, where I specialized in innovative reaction methodologies and uncovering reaction mechanisms using computational and spectroscopic approaches.

My breakthrough observation of the π-complex intermediate during arenes nitration led to the development of an efficient HNO3/HFIP nitrating system. I also pioneered the Monomer-Polymer-Monomer (MPM) method—revolutionizing recyclable polymer creation and the synthesis of valuable 5‑substituted benzofurans for pharmaceutical applications.

In 2022, I joined TSMC as a scientist and project manager to tackle complex material challenges in advanced nodes, significantly enhancing N3 yield. To date, I have published 11 papers and hold multiple patents.
