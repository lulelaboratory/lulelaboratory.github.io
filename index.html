Summary

I work at the intersection of chemistry, computation, and semiconductor manufacturing. After earning my PhD in Chemistry from Tsinghua University, I joined TSMC in 2022 to tackle complex material challenges at advanced technology nodes.

During my PhD in Professor Ruimao Hua's laboratory (2015–2021), I developed new reaction methodologies and studied their mechanisms with computational chemistry and spectroscopy. I directly observed the π-complex intermediate in arene nitration, which led to the efficient HNO3/HFIP room-temperature nitrating system. I also invented the Monomer–Polymer–Monomer (MPM) method, enabling recyclable polymers and the synthesis of 5-substituted benzofurans relevant to the pharmaceutical industry.

Today my focus is AI for Science and CMP simulation: applying deep learning, molecular simulation, and data-driven modeling to understand material behavior at advanced nodes. I have 11 published papers and hold multiple patents as the inventor.

MD Fragment Analyzer

Python tool for fragment analysis of molecular dynamics trajectories. Computes interatomic distances, detects bonds from covalent / van der Waals radii with a user-configurable scale factor, and groups atoms into fragments via a union-find algorithm. Supports periodic boundary conditions, Numba JIT acceleration, multiprocessing, and optional CUDA GPU acceleration, with a Tkinter GUI.
