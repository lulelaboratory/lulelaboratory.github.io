About Me

I earned my Ph.D. in Chemistry from Tsinghua University. From 2015 to 2021, I was a pivotal member of Professor Ruimao Hua’s laboratory, where I specialized in innovative reaction methodologies and uncovering reaction mechanisms through computational and spectroscopic approaches.

My work led to a groundbreaking observation of the π-complex as the key intermediate in the nitration of arenes at room temperature, culminating in the efficient HNO3/HFIP nitrating system for arenes. I also pioneered the Monomer-Polymer-Monomer (MPM) method—revolutionizing recyclable polymer creation and the synthesis of valuable 5-substituted benzofurans for the pharmaceutical industry.

In 2022, I joined Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC) as a scientist and project manager, tackling complex material challenges in advanced nodes and significantly enhancing N3 yield. To date, I have published 11 papers in esteemed international journals and hold multiple patents.
